Shipping and Returns

Shipping Information:

We strive to ensure swift delivery of your purr-chases. Here's what you need to know:

  • We offer worldwide shipping to bring joy to cats and their humans around the globe.
  • Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days, but expedited options are available for those who can't wait to spoil their furballs.
  • Please ensure accurate shipping information during checkout to prevent your package from going on an unexpected adventure.

Returns and Exchanges:

We want you and your cat to be delighted with your Arya Kart experience. If for any reason you need to return or exchange an item, here's the scoop:

  • Unopened and unused items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
  • Opened items that didn't spark joy for your cat can be exchanged or refunded, minus a nominal "catnip testing fee."
  • Please contact our customer service team for return authorization and instructions.
